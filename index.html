15 lbs. Oven-Roasted-Spatchcock Turkey

Weis Markets sponsered by the Penn State University want to do something very special for this Thanksgiving by giving out a chef's trick meal that saves time without sacrificing flavor.

$29.99

Coming soon

Step 1. Preheat

Preheat the oven to 425 ℉.

Step 2. Rip It Apart

Cut the backbone off and rip open the turkey from the bottom of it slightly.

Step 3. Press It Down

Press the turkey down facing upwards until you hear the wishbone crack.

Step 4. Lining

Line up the turkey on top of a baking sheet and wrap it with heavy duty foil. Make sure you place a wire rack on top of the foil.

Step 5. Seasoning Blend

Mix kosher salt with dark brown sugar, ground black pepper, rubbed sage, ground thyme, chili powder, smoked paprika, granulated garlic, and onion powder to make seasoning blend.

Step 6. Seasoning

Dry the turkey and rub it with 1/4 cup of olive oil. Season the whole turkey with the seasoning blend.

Step 7. Roasting

Place the turkey in the oven for 30 minutes and decrease the temperature to 350 ℉.

Step 8. Brushing

Take the turkey out of the oven after it's done, and brush it with the remaining 2 tbsp. of olive oil.
